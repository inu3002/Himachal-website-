Traditional Food of Himachal Pradesh

District-wise traditional dishes & cultural flavors

Shimla

Dishes: Madra, Siddu, Chha Gosht

Traditional Pahadi food rich in spices and dairy products.

Kangra

Dishes: Kangri Dham, Madra, Mittha

Famous for Dham cuisine served in traditional functions.

Kullu

Dishes: Siddu, Aktori, Trout Fish

Mountain cuisine with wheat, butter and local herbs.

Mandi

Dishes: Dham Thali, Madra, Kadhi

Famous for temple food culture and traditional meals.

Chamba

Dishes: Chana Madra, Sattu, Sepu Badi

Ancient recipes with local grains and spices.

Lahaul & Spiti

Dishes: Thukpa, Momos, Butter Tea

Tibetan influence food and high-altitude cuisine.

Kinnaur

Dishes: Buckwheat dishes, Apple products

Tribal food culture with organic mountain crops.

Solan

Dishes: Siddu, Dham, Pickles

Blend of traditional and modern food culture.

Una

Dishes: Paratha, Lassi, Pahadi curry

Plain-region influence with Punjabi flavors.

Bilaspur

Dishes: Fish curry, Dham

Lake-region food culture with local spices.

Hamirpur

Dishes: Siddu, Madra, Traditional roti

Simple traditional food culture.

Sirmaur

Dishes: Ghee-based dishes, Madra

Temple food traditions and village cuisine.
